In Helbreath Ghost the apocalypse mobs have some special changes. We change all the normal monster in apocalypse for "Apoc" mobs. They are more strongs and with a high health.
But why we did it? Is simple, we just do it because we want a fun and interesting hunt and pvp server.

1° In Helbreath Friends the Apocalypse Gate open every sunday at 00:00 o'clock, in Promise Land with a Dynamic Gate.

2° Maps: Druncian City, Infernia A, Infernia B, maze, Procella, Abaddon. When Dynamic Gate are open you can go to DruncnCity, and choose teleport to Infernia A or Infernia B, or also can hunt in DruncnCity to Hellclaw and Nizie...

3° When you choose Infernia A or B, you need kill all the npc on the map and when the map are empty, open  a other Dynamic Gate  at the empty map to tp for maze map.

4° Maze map is a neutral map, you dont need clear the map, you only need go to the next Dynamic Gate to tp to Procella.

5° Procella Map, big hunt are waiting for you and your friends, 600+ mobs on that map (Stalker, Master Mage Orc, Tentocle, Giant Plant and others), here you also need to clear the map, to open a new Dynamic Gate, you the map are empty and summon a boss (Death Wyvern, you dont need to kill that mob to tp fot Abaddon map.)

6° Abaddon Map, here also have a big hunt waiting for you, need to clear all map (Hellclaw, Tigerworm, Fire-Wyvern, Wyvern, Barlog, Gagoyle, and others.) When your team clear the map, a new mensage show on your client  "Abbadon has appeared", now you can finally kill the Abaddon, when you kill the Abaddon the Apocalypse map are closed.
