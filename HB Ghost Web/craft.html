In order to upgrade a necklace, or create a gemware, you need to have a Crafting Bowl bought from the Shop.

Craft of Magin Gem's


There are 4 different types of Magic Gem's, with each different effect. As you can see in the table, the following Magic gems do;

  • Magic Diamond: Adds hitting probability (HP) to magic and physical attacks when equipped
  • Magic Ruby: Adds health points (HP) regen when equipped
  • Magic Sapphire: Adds physical absorption (PA) when equipped
  • Magic Emerald: Gives the wearer extra magic absorption (MA) when equipped
